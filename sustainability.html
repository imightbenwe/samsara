Our Commitment

Preserving Our Planet

At Samsara Sanctuary, we believe that true luxury includes responsibility toward our environment. Every decision we make—from construction materials to daily operations—considers our impact on the pristine Koh Samui coastline and surrounding ecosystems.

Our Initiatives

Renewable Energy

100% of our energy comes from solar panels and wind turbines. We generate 85% of our power on-site, with excess energy supplied to the local community.

Water Conservation

Advanced rainwater harvesting collects 2 million liters annually. Our greywater system recycles 100% of non-contaminated water for landscaping.

Organic Farming

Our 3-hectare organic farm produces 70% of our fresh vegetables and fruits. Zero pesticides, composted waste returns nutrients to soil.

Zero Waste Program

Our comprehensive waste management system achieves 94% diversion from landfills through composting, recycling, and reuse programs.

Marine Protection

We maintain a 2km marine sanctuary protecting coral reefs. Monthly beach cleanups remove over 500kg of ocean-sourced debris annually.

Community Partnership

60% of our staff are local Thai citizens. We fund 5 scholarships annually for underprivileged local students in environmental studies.
